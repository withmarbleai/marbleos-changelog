# Workspace Switching

You can now click "Open Folder" in the workspace dropdown to manually select a folder on your computer to open. After you open a folder this way, Marble will remember it as a workspace, and you'll see it in the workspace dropdown. 

If Marble isn't showing your workspace in the dropdown yet (e.g., maybe it's in Desktop), you will have to open that folder once for Marble to remember it as a workspace.
